    Weekly Income Above 3000, Male vs Female

    The 3D map below shows the relationship between total number of people and the number of people earning above 3000 dollars per week, females represented as on the left and males represented as right. The color represent the total number of people in that region, while the height represent the number of people earning above 3000 dollars per week.









    From this map we can clearly see that the number of male earning above 3000 dollars per week is larger the number of female in Sydney.
