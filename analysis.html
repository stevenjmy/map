    Analysis

    Urban Transformation is a response to redevelopment strategies meeting all requirements for the future. This not only includes the changing of population mobility from one place to another, providing new jobs in a certain area, and improving living quality for the public who lives in the certain region, but also incorporates integrating diverse community's cultural, making a vibrant city with full of life and characters, and improving environment into its future design.
